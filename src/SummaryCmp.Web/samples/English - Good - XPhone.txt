Technical product description with features
X-Phone Connect is the communication software for maximum flexibility. Whether you're working from home, traveling for business or hot desking at the office, the X-Phone Connect people can always get hold of you using your office number automatically. It works as a soft phone over VoIP on your laptop or via a mobile app. The Any Device feature lets you use whatever device suits you. landline, smartphone, anything really. XPhone Connect is sold through a network of certified resellers across Germany, Austria, Switzerland and France. So you get personal advice and reliable support from local experts.
